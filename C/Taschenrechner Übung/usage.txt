Usage:

  ueb01 -h

    shows this help and exits.

  - or -

  ueb01 NUM1 OP NUM2

    computes a binary integer operation and prints the result. A call consists
    of a number followed by an arithmetic operation and another number.

    NUM1 and NUM2 have to be positive integer numbers (including 0).

    OP is one of:
      + adds NUM1 and NUM2
      - subtracts NUM2 from NUM1
      * multiplies NUM1 and NUM2
      / divides NUM1 by NUM2 (integer result)
      % divides NUM1 by NUM2 (integer remainder)
